At InTextify, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services at www.intextify.com.

a. Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you provide when you register, contact support, or otherwise interact with our services.

b. Text Content You Provide

2. Authentication & Storage

We use Supabase for authentication and user management. Your email and a hashed password are stored securely by Supabase. You can request deletion of your account at any time by contacting us at privacy@intextify.com.
